Ingredients

  • Burger bun
  • Beef patty (or alternative like chicken, veggie patty, etc.)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Prepare Patty: Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook Patty: Grill or pan-sear the patties over medium-high heat until cooked to desired doneness (about 4-5 minutes per side for medium).
  3. Prepare Bun: Slice burger buns in half and toast lightly on the grill or in a skillet.
  4. Assemble Burger: Place cooked patty on the bottom half of the bun. Layer with lettuce, tomato slices, cheese, and pickles.
  5. Serve: Place the top half of the bun on top of the burger and serve immediately.
